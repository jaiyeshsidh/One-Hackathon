One Hack-a-thon

The world is going through the worst time currently in the wake of covid19 pandemic and if researchers are to be believed, there would be more pandemics coming our way. More than ever, there is a strong need to have us all stand together and fight these unknown, unseen and unheard-of enemies together.

One Hack-a-thon is an initiative designed to encourage developers, startups, technology students, management students, technology experts to come together and to create ideas and solutions which could potentially help us braze these attacks in future in a better manner.
